Fun Animal Facts

Welcome to my website! Here are a couple of fun and interesting animal facts!

Animal Facts Animal Images

Grizzly Bear

The Grizzly bear is a large, carnivorous predator that can be found throughout America. Their big, lumbering bodies are covered in brown fur and they have huge paws with sharp claws that can grow up to 4 inches long.

Grizzly bears live off of a diet consisting of fish, meat, berries and plants.

Caribou

The caribou is a large herbivore that can be found in places such as Antarctica, North America, Siberia, and Europe.

Caribou are sturdy, hoofed mammals who have a brown pelt and impressive antlers that can reach up to 51 inches long on male caribou!

Out of all deer-species, the caribou has the largest antlers relative to their body size!

Cougar

The cougar is a carnivorous predator that lives in many regions across America, though seeing one in person is quite rare as they are very elusive!

Cougars have a tan coat that is a brown and reddish in color, with a long black-tipped tail.

Using their powerful hind legs, cougars can jump 18 feet vertically and leap 40 to 45 feet horizontally!

Although cougars can't roar like most big cats, they are capable of purring.

Bumblebee

The bumblebee is a fuzzy insect that is found throughout the northern hemisphere. They have big bodies, little wings, and a dinstinct black and yellow patterning.

While bumblebees do make honey, they eat it instead of stockpiling it like honeybees, since bumblebee workers do not survive the winter.
