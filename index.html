Grizzly Bear

The Grizzly bear is a large, carnivorous predator that lives in various places across America. With big, lumbering bodies and huge paws with sharp claws, grizzly bears live off of a diet consisting of fish, meat, berries and plants.

Caribou

The caribou is a large herbivore that lives in many different continents such as Antarctica, North America, Siberia and Europe.

Cougar

The cougar is a carnivorous predator that can be found all across America.

Bumblebee

The bumblebee is a fuzzy insect that is found throughout the northern hemisphere. They live in colonies with one queen
